About & Skills

I'm Bryan Hinostroza — producer, beatmaker and sonic architect operating under the alias DIBRY. Born and trained in Ecuador, now based in Brisbane, Australia, I build tracks that live at the intersection of Latin street culture and contemporary production aesthetics.

From writing beats in my bedroom to directing vocal sessions for artists with Spotify placements, every record I touch gets the same obsessive attention to groove, space and emotion. I don't just make tracks — I build the sonic identity of the artists I work with.
